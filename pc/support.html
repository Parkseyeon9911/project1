  • WHAT MEANS HD?

    High Definition means highest quality, both in terms of optics and mechanical measures. It features special fluoride glass materials and includes AquaDura® coating. Ultravid HD focusing remains fully functional, even in extreme temperatures – with unerringly smooth action.

  • WHAT IS THE EXIT PUPIL?

    The bright, circular field that can be seen by looking through the eyepiece from a distance of around 30 cm is called exit pupil. It is calculated by dividing the front lens diameter by the magnification. A sign of quality: the circular field should always be perfectly round with sharp edges.

  • WHAT MEANS ER i?

    ER stands for extended eye relief. ‘i’ is for ‘illuminated’, designating illumination suitable for twilight use.

  • WHAT MEANS EHR?

    EHR stands for Equivalent Horizontal Range, a helpful parameter for shots in mountainous terrain. A special Leica algorithm always takes at least one ballistic trajectory into account when calculating the EHR, providing results far more accurate than the ‘rifleman’s rule’.

  • WHAT IS THE AUTOMATIC POWER-OFF FUNCTION?

    Via acceleration and position sensors, this intelligent technology switches off the illuminated dot when the rifle is not in shooting position. It also extends battery life considerably.

  • WHAT MEANS ASPH. ?

    This abbreviation means that an aspherical lens is used in the optical system. Light rays passing through the edges of the aspherical lens are refracted differently – this results in perfect edge-to-edge sharpness across the entire field of view.

  • WHAT MEANS HD?

    High Definition means highest quality, both in terms of optics and mechanical measures. It features special fluoride glass materials and includes AquaDura® coating. Ultravid HD focusing remains fully functional, even in extreme temperatures – with unerringly smooth action.

  • WHAT IS THE AQUADURA® COATING?

    Its water and dirt repellent properties guarantee a clear view – even in unfavourable weather conditions. AquaDura® improves viewing comfort and the increased abrasion resistance protects the precious optical elements even more effectively against damage.

  • WHAT MEANS HLS®?

    HLS® ist the HighLux-System, used in Duovid and Ultravid binoculars. It can significantly increase transmission, contrast and sharpness. It is a system of mechanical measures for the reduction of stray light (for example baffles, anti-reflective paints) in combination with special reflective coating of prisms.
